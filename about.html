My name is Samyukta, but my friends call me Samy. I’m a 4th year undergraduate at UC Berkeley double majoring in Computer Science and Integrative Biology. I came into college intending to study developmental genetics but once I took my first CS class, I was hooked!

I grew up in Dubai and was never exposed to Computer Science, so I had never opened Terminal in my life before that class. Despite the steep learning curve, I quickly fell in love with the CS way of thinking. Computer Science challenges me to think hard about things and actually find solutions to problems, and I love the creativity it inspires in me! I can work on a project for hours and not even realize how much time has gone by simply because I'm so engrossed in it. Recently, I've been thinking about what I want to do once I graduate and I've realized that I would love to work in the tech industry and continue solving problems and coming up with creative solutions!

When I’m not in class or frantically finishing a problem set, I like to design things too. Doodling used to be a hobby of mine, but now I’ve been experimenting with other media and it’s been a whole lot of fun! I recently bought the iPad Pro and Apple Pencil and I'm absolutely nuts about them!

Aside from school, I'm passionate about exploring the world (I've lived in 4 countries and counting!), working out, food, music, and narrowing the gender gap! Check out my projects or reach out and say hi!
